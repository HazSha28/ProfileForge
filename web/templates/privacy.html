Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

ProfileForge is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of the individuals whose data you process through our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use ProfileForge.

By using the ProfileForge service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with its terms, please do not use the service. For questions, contact us at support@profileforge.io.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information in connection with the service:

Account Information

  • Name and email address when you register with an email/password.
  • OAuth profile data (name, email, profile picture URL) when you sign in via Google or GitHub.
  • Password (stored as a hashed value — we never store plain-text passwords).

Uploaded Content

  • Recruiter CSV files and resume PDF files you upload for processing.
  • Platform profile URLs you provide (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.).
  • JSON configuration text you enter in the service.
  • Transformed candidate profile output generated by the pipeline.

Usage Data

  • Log data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Session data stored in cookies or server-side session storage.
  • API request logs including endpoints called, response times, and error codes.

How We Use Your Information

ProfileForge uses the information we collect to:

  • Provide the service — parse, normalise, merge, score, and return candidate profile data.
  • Manage your account — authenticate you, maintain your session, and communicate service-related updates.
  • Send transactional emails — welcome emails, password reset requests, and important service notices.
  • Improve the service — analyse aggregate usage patterns to identify and fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Ensure security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Comply with legal obligations — respond to lawful requests from authorities and meet our regulatory responsibilities.

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing profiling, and we do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Information Sharing

ProfileForge does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating the service (e.g., hosting, email delivery), subject to confidentiality obligations and prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of rights — when necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of ProfileForge, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, provided the successor entity honors the commitments made in this policy.
  • With your consent — in any other circumstances with your explicit prior consent.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Account data — retained until you close your account, plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal and compliance purposes.
  • Uploaded files (CSV, PDF) — processed in memory or temporary storage and deleted immediately after the pipeline completes. We do not retain uploaded candidate files beyond the duration of a single processing request.
  • Pipeline output — not stored by ProfileForge server-side by default; the JSON output is returned directly to your browser session.
  • Usage logs — retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support@profileforge.io.

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS (HTTPS).
  • Hashed storage of passwords using a strong one-way hashing algorithm.
  • Session management with server-side signed cookies and configurable expiry.
  • OAuth 2.0 for third-party authentication (no password stored for OAuth users).
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to notify us immediately of any suspected unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right of erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — request a structured, machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@profileforge.io. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).

Cookies

ProfileForge uses cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the service.

  • Session cookies — essential cookies used to maintain your authenticated session. These are deleted when you close your browser or log out.
  • "Remember me" cookies — persistent cookies set when you opt in to stay signed in for 30 days.
  • CSRF protection cookies — used to protect form submissions from cross-site request forgery attacks.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling essential session cookies will prevent you from using the authenticated features of the service.

Third-Party Services

ProfileForge integrates with the following third-party identity providers for OAuth-based authentication:

Google OAuth

When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name, email address, and profile picture URL. We use this information solely to create or authenticate your ProfileForge account. Your use of Google Sign-In is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

GitHub OAuth

When you sign in with GitHub, we receive your GitHub account name, email address, and public profile information. We use this information solely to create or authenticate your ProfileForge account. Your use of GitHub Sign-In is also subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

We do not receive or store your OAuth provider passwords. ProfileForge only requests the minimum scopes necessary to identify and authenticate you.

Children's Privacy

ProfileForge is not directed to individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately at support@profileforge.io and we will take prompt steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Sending an email notification to the address associated with your account.
  • Displaying a notice within the service at your next login.

Your continued use of the service after the effective date of the updated policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The current version is always available at profileforge.io/privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@profileforge.io
  • Product: ProfileForge — Multi-Source Candidate Data Transformer

We will endeavor to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days. For data subject requests under GDPR or CCPA, we will respond within the timeframe required by law (typically 30 days, extendable by an additional 60 days in complex cases).
